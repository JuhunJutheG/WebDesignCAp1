About The GardenShop.ie...

1995 Our founding father, Zac Affron established The Garden Shop Limited to help nearby gardening communities. He started the business with only 3 people, Shia Labouf, Adam Sandler and Chris Brown.

1998 The Garden Shop ltd became well known, trusted company within county Meath. Employees at the time were 50.

2001 Our founder, Zac Affron decided to expand the business from explicitly offline to on/offline, which led The Garden Shop to Ireland's sensation.

2008 After global economy crisis, we were under such high pressure to lay off employees... However, Zac wasn't giving up on anyone. He sold his own house to pay employees and slept in the office for few years.

2009 Our research team successfully created genetically modified plants, which enabled any plants to grow in different environment. The chief of the research team, 'Cillian Murphy' received the Nobel prize.

2015 The Garden Shop became a world best garden shop and Zac was on the front Time magazine for top 5 the most influential people

2024 The Garden Shop is still serving gardening communities and anyone who is interested in gardening.
